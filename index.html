KALIX

v1.0 · Hardware Auditor · Windows 64-bit

Available Now

One portable executable that tells you exactly what is running inside your machine — and whether it should be trusted. Kernel-level truth. No abstraction. No network calls. No compromise.

Kernel-Level Component Verification
Reads directly from hardware enumeration APIs — bypassing OS abstraction. CPU, GPU, RAM, NVMe, motherboard. Serial cross-reference detects cloned or tampered components instantly.
Real-Time Thermal Intelligence
Direct sensor-bus polling at 100ms intervals. Junction temperature, TDP headroom, clock frequency, and voltage state. Throttle events captured with full system snapshots.
Integrity Verification
SHA-256 manifest written on every run — cryptographically binding the audit to exact hardware state. Deterministic. Reproducible. Immutable.
Structured JSON Export
Every audit produces a structured JSON manifest. Integrate with Grafana, Prometheus, Elastic, or any pipeline you already run.

Investment Thesis

We back deep-tech from the inside. Capital and engineering talent into the infrastructure layer.

Early-stage startup · Not a regulated investment vehicle · This page describes our philosophy, not a formal fund offering

Aotsuki is not just a product company. We invest capital and engineering talent into the infrastructure layer of technology — the part most investors don't understand because they've never built it.

Most investment in tech targets the application layer: consumer apps, SaaS, AI wrappers. We invest in what those products run on — hardware intelligence, kernel tooling, network primitives, distributed consensus protocols. These are the assets that compound over decades.

Infrastructure-First

We invest below the application layer — hardware intelligence, kernel tooling, network primitives, distributed consensus. Applications come and go. Infrastructure stays and compounds.

Long-Horizon Thinking

10-year investment horizons. Deep-tech compounds slowly and defensibly. We don't optimise for growth charts — we optimise for durability, defensibility, and engineering correctness.

Operator-Led Due Diligence

We read source code, profile hardware, and stress-test architecture before committing capital. No surface-level pitch deck evaluation. If we can't understand the technology, we don't invest.

Collective Intelligence

Every investment thesis is subject to collective engineering scrutiny. We don't move on consensus alone — we move when the engineering evidence is compelling. Speed from clarity, not urgency.
